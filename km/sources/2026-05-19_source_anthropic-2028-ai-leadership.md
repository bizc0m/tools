# Anthropic 2028 AI leadership scenarios

## Summary
Anthropic argues that global AI leadership by 2028 depends on whether democracies preserve a compute and frontier-model lead over authoritarian regimes, especially the CCP.

## Raw Input
https://www.anthropic.com/research/2028-ai-leadership?utm_source=tldrai

## Key Points
- Anthropic presents two 2028 scenarios: democracies keep a commanding AI lead, or the CCP catches up and shapes AI norms.
- The article frames compute access as the core constraint for frontier AI leadership.
- It argues for tighter export controls, closing loopholes around offshore compute access, and disrupting model distillation attacks.
- Anthropic says a 12-24 month lead by democracies could improve safety, governance and strategic stability.
- The article links AI leadership to national security, repression, military balance, cyber capability and global infrastructure.
- It supports AI safety dialogue with Chinese experts, but says such dialogue works better if democracies maintain a capability lead.
